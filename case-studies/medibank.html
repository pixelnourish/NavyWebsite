Healthbook is part of the Australian Government's focus on eHealth services

Medibank won a multi-million dollar government contract to create a pilot version of the government's eHealth record. We worked with Medibank to design the Healthbook system.

Project summary

 
Audience Average age of 75 with chronic illness
 
Technology Web based software

What we did

We created a strategic vision for Healthbook, and then completed detailed user experience design of every screen.

The result

Medibank easily met their government targets for signups.

In Australia, people's health records are stored at their individual providers.

For example, heart test results are stored with the cardiologist and prescriptions are stored at the pharmacy.

Healthbook was created to bring all of those medical records together in one place.

The goal is to make sure the right people have the right information at the right time.

To test the Healthbook concept we designed a pilot version for a particularly challenging audience.
 

The average age of participants in the pilot was 73 years old.

They suffered from a variety of chronic health conditions
