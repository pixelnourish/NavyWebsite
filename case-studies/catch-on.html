Catch On! is a mobile game to teach kids about sustainable fishing

Most people who fish in Victoria are seasoned anglers. But Fisheries Victoria wanted to engage with a younger demographic. They asked us to visualise an iPad game that could help them do it.

"Catch On!" was our concept for a game that's fun, educational and uniquely Victorian.
