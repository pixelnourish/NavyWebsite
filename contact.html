At what stage of a project should I get in touch?

The earlier the better! Ideally, we'd love to chat before the brief or the tender has been written. These sorts of documents can have a huge effect on the outcome of your project, and we love helping to guide them in the right direction, whichever supplier you choose.

Are you hiring and can I send my resume?

We're always open to hearing from talented people who are interested in working with us. We put a big emphasis on teamwork, so we'd particularly like to hear from proven teams as well as individual people. Send us your resume.

Are you available for speaking engagements?

We conduct a limited number of speaking engagements on design theory each year. Please contact us for details.
